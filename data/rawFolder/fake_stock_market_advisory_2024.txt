Title: MHA I4C Advisory - Beware of Fake Stock Market Investment Websites and Apps (April 2024)

With the rising adoption of digital platforms like Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, and Telegram, cybercriminals are increasingly luring citizens into fake stock market investments leading to severe financial losses.

Modus Operandi:
- Sponsored advertisements featuring deepfake videos and images of prominent individuals are run on social media to redirect users to fraudulent WhatsApp or Telegram trading groups.
- Fraudsters share links to fake trading applications (APKs or unofficial app store links) and provide Indian mule bank accounts for depositing funds.
- Initial small payouts are simulated to build trust, after which communication is abruptly cut off once large sums are deposited.

Precautions:
- Exercise extreme caution regarding sponsored investment ads on social media.
- Never share sensitive financial details or login credentials with unverified entities.
- Verify whether an Investment Advisor is officially registered with SEBI before engaging. Report cyber frauds at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
