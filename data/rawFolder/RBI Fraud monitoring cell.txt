PRESS RELEASE
June 5, 2013
RESERVE BANK OF INDIA
RBI's Fraud Monitoring Cell to function from Bengaluru from July 1[cite: 21]

The Reserve Bank of India's Fraud Monitoring Cell (attached to the Department of Banking Supervision, Central Office) shifted from the World Trade Centre, Mumbai, to the Bengaluru Regional Office of the Reserve Bank, effective July 1, 2013[cite: 21].
