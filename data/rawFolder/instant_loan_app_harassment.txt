DOCUMENT ID: RBI-I4C-ADV-005
TITLE: Advisory on Unauthorized Digital Lending Apps and Harassment Practices
ISSUING AGENCY: Reserve Bank of India (RBI) & Cyber Crime Division
DATE OF ISSUE: Current Operating Version

1. OVERVIEW OF THE FRAUD
Unregistered and illegal digital lending applications offer instant, paperless short-term loans but trap borrowers in predatory debt cycles and severe harassment.

2. MODUS OPERANDI
- Invasive Permissions: Upon installation, these apps harvest unauthorized access to the user's contacts, photo gallery, media files, and location data.
- Exorbitant Charges: Hidden fees, excessive processing charges, and undisclosed high-interest rates lead to massive debt accumulation within days.
- Public Shaming and Blackmail: When borrowers default or seek time, recovery agents use morphed images, abusive messages, and broadcast threats to the borrower's entire contact list.

3. PREVENTIVE MEASURES & OFFICIAL STANCE
- Borrow exclusively from regulated entities such as banks and Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs) registered with the RBI.
- Verify the list of authorized digital lending apps published on the official RBI website.
- Do not grant excessive permissions to mobile applications, especially access to contacts and media storage.
