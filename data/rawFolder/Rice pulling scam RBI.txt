PRESS RELEASE
July 31, 2018
RESERVE BANK OF INDIA
Cautionary advice on Rice pulling Scam[cite: 19, 20]

It has come to the notice of the RBI that unscrupulous persons are marketing a device termed "Rice Puller" made of copper/iridium, claimed to possess magical properties of attracting grains of rice[cite: 20]. Associated persons have misrepresented Government Securities auction circulars and notifications issued by the RBI/GoI as proof of RBI sourcing funds for such activities[cite: 20]. 

The RBI cautions the public not to fall prey to such claims, noting that associating with such persons results in direct financial losses, and advises the public to refrain from responding and report such incidents to investigating authorities[cite: 20].
