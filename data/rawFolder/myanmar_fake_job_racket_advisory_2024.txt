Title: Embassy of India Yangon - Advisory Regarding Fake Job Rackets Targeting Indian Youth (May 2024)

An increasing number of Indian nationals are falling victim to international crime syndicates operating in the Myawaddy region along the Myanmar-Thailand border, with new trafficking hubs emerging in areas like Hpa Lu. 

Advisory Guidelines:
- Indian nationals must avoid accepting unverified overseas job offers floated via social media platforms or dubious agents.
- The credentials of foreign employers and recruiting agencies must be thoroughly verified through the respective Indian Embassy before undertaking any travel or job commitments abroad.
- For Myanmar-related assistance or verification queries, the Embassy of India in Yangon can be contacted via email (cons.yangon@mea.gov.in) or mobile (+9595419602).
